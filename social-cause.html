Social Cause

Your knowledge can have a butterfly effect and create waves of change for India’s underserved. You help us help others—see how.

Play for a larger Cause

Knowledge has the power to bring progress and prosperity to every member of society. This is what inspired us to launch QuizMaster. When you quiz with us, we celebrate your curiosity and reward your knowledge. But is that enough?

There are thousands of bright young minds in some of India’s most vulnerable, underserved populations. We want to open the doors of learning and bring them educational opportunities that will improve their quality of life. That’s why we donate a part of our earnings to our NGO partners who do stellar work in grassroots education for young Indians all over the country.

Teach for India

The Teach for India Fellowship is a movement to eliminate educational inequity in India. They identify bright, highly motivated youth from India’s best colleges and workplaces and give them the chance to teach young children in under-resourced schools. Experiencing the grassroot reality of India’s education system inspires and equips them to make a difference through leadership roles in and beyond education.
