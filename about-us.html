The QuizMaster Story

Our mission is to celebrate curiosity, encourage learning, and reward knowledge through thrilling quiz experiences.

Quiz Master is a mobile quizzing app

We are a nation of curious minds, constantly absorbing facts & knowledge through online reading, conversations, videos, news reports, and experiences.

Knowledge is certainly power and we at QuizMaster wondered: can it also be money? So we created the QuizMaster app to reward the pursuit of knowledge by giving out big, instant cash rewards.

Why real money quizzing?

We Indians love mobile gaming. We also love quizzing, be it inter-school competitions or celebrity game shows on TV. But quizzes with big prizes come with many entry barriers.

Through the free-for-all QuizMaster app, we want to bring the joy of quizzing to every well-read, curious Indian. And along with it, the chance to turn knowledge into big, real money rewards!

What To Expect

Open to everyone

Students, professionals, homemakers, retirees, anyone can sign up & quiz with us.

Wide range of topics

Our questions cover sports, GK, entertainment, current affairs, culture, literature, science & more.

Reward for skill, not luck

Answer correctly within the stipulated time and stand a chance to win.

Fun, easy format

We ask multiple choice questions with rich text/visual clues to help you along—because we want you to win!

100% secure

We’re built on a tamper-proof blockchain architecture and use AI to track responses and break ties.

Get QM coins for the fastest correct answer.

Get QM coins for every correct answer. 1 QM coin = ₹6. Use these to enter daily/weekly quizzes and win real cash rewards.

We’re about more than gaming and entertainment. We believe deeply in the power of learning to bring prosperity to every Indian, which is why we give generously to the cause of education.
